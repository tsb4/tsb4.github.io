About me

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Centre INRIA de l'Université de Rennes with Prof. Shadi Ibrahim. I had a PhD in 2025 at Université Côte d'Azur, supervised by Prof. Frederic Giroire and Prof. Ramon Aparicio-Pardo. During my PhD, I worked on optimizing energy consumption and model performance in Machine Learning inference. My research focuses on developing algorithms for scheduling ML inference requests on cloud systems along with model compression. I also worked on estimating ML models energy consumption for different tasks and projecting the enrgy gains by applying model selection.
